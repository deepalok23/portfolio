Deep Alok is a lyricist, multi-genre rapper, music producer, and mixing & mastering engineer from India — making music Dil Se (from the heart). His journey began in 2022 with "It's Freestyle" — a raw, unfiltered introduction to the world — and has since grown into a diverse body of work spanning over 18 tracks.

His artistry spans the full sonic spectrum: from the cinematic rage of UK Drill and Trap ("Panne Faad", "Chale Naam") to the introspective weight of Lofi Rap and R&B ("Shaayad Mai", "3:00 AM", "Sapne"). Each release is a chapter — immersive, intentional, and deeply personal.

As a music creator, Deep Alok handles everything — writing, producing in FL Studio, and mastering with professional tools like Serum, FabFilter, Ozone 12, and Omnisphere 3. His recent work like "Sky High", "Hara Firbhi", and "Udaan" continues to push his sound into new melodic territories.

2022 · DISCOVERY
Began exploring the world of music — discovering genres, studying artists, building an ear for sound, and gaining foundational knowledge of music theory and production basics.
2023 · FIRST STEPS
Took the leap and officially started his journey as an independent artist. Wrote his first lyrics, stepped into the recording booth, and released his debut track — putting his voice out into the world for the first time.
2024 · PRODUCTION & CRAFT
Leveled up significantly — diving deep into music production, mixing, and mastering. Started crafting his own beats from scratch, developing a distinct sonic identity, and releasing more polished, self-produced records.
2025 · GROWTH & FREELANCING
Expanded beyond personal projects — offering freelance music production, mixing & mastering services to other artists. Continued releasing new music while steadily growing his audience and refining his craft.
